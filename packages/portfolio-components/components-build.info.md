# **Profile Portfolio Landing Page Structure (Approved & Production-Ready)**

### **1. Hero**

* Name + Profession
* Short value statement
* Primary CTA (Hire Me / View Work)
* Secondary CTA (Download CV / Contact)
* Strong visual (photo / illustration / animation)

---

### **2. Achievements / Social Proof**

* Company logos
* Featured in (publications / podcasts / mentions)
* Quick achievement metrics:

  * 5+ years experience
  * 40+ completed projects
  * Worked with global teams

---

### **3. Specializations / What I Do**

* Business-oriented skill categories
* Clear labels (Web App Development, UI/UX, Frontend Engineering, Branding)
* Minimal icons or card list

---

### **4. Featured Projects**

* Title
* 1-line problem â†’ solution
* Tags (React, UX, AI, API, etc.)
* Visual / thumbnail
* CTA: View Case Study

Case Study (optional page/window):

* Problem
* Your role
* Tech stack
* Before â†’ After
* Outcomes / metrics

---

### **5. Skills / Tech Stack**

Divided into:

* Frontend
* Backend
* Tools & Design Tools
* Additional skills (Testing, DevOps, CI/CD, Architecture)

Clear, concise, and categorized.

---

### **6. Testimonials / Client Reviews**

Card format:

* Client name + role
* Quote
* Project type
* Optional rating

Builds massive trust.

---

### **7. About Me**

Includes:

* Who you are
* What motivates you
* What kind of work you enjoy
* Mission / philosophy
* Professional but friendly photo

Gives personality to the portfolio.

---

### **8. Services (If Freelancing)**

For high-conversion freelancing portfolios:

* Services you offer
* Deliverables included
* Who this service is ideal for
* Benefits
* Optional pricing tiers (Starter / Pro / Enterprise)

---

### **9. Support My Work (Optional but Nice Add-On)**

Examples:

* Buy Me a Coffee
* GitHub Sponsor
* Ko-Fi
* Patreon
* How people can contribute to your projects

This is great if:

* You are open source focused
* You publish content
* You build tools/components publicly

---

### **10. Contact CTA**

Strong call-to-action block:

* Email
* Contact form
* Social links (GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Dribbble, etc.)
* Optional: Calendly booking link

Should be visually strong to encourage action.

---

### **11. Footer**

Includes:

* Navigation
* Social icons
* Copyright
* Email link
* Optional: link to portfolio source on GitHub
